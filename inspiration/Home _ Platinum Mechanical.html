About Platinum Mechanical, LLC

Platinum Mechanical, LLC is a full-service Mechanical Contracting Company that started in March, 2011. The company performs large commercial, industrial , & institutional piping, plumbing, sheet metal, controls and utilities projects in Tulsa and its surrounding areas. Platinum has a large workforce of highly trained and highly motivated employees.

Our Projects

Platinum Mechanical, LLC

Platinum Mechanical, LLC has installed mechanical and plumbing systems in many of Northeast Oklahoma’s well-known buildings including St. Francis Hospital, St. John’s Hospital, Hillcrest Hospital, the Osage & Cherokee Casinos, the OSU Center for Health Sciences, as well as facilities at The University of Tulsa, Oklahoma State University, Northeastern Oklahoma State University, Tulsa Community College, Union Public Schools, Jenks Public Schools, and Broken Arrow Public Schools to name just a few. We have been trusted to work on several historic buildings in downtown Tulsa including renovations at the Fox Hotel and the Universal Ford Building and we also completed the mechanical construction on the first LEED certified project downtown.
